Who We Are

Since its inception, SANAV SOFTWARE has been committed to innovation, continuous learning, and delivering high-quality digital solutions. The company specializes in software development, web applications, IT consulting, and digital transformation services.

With a strong focus on modern technologies and customer-centric implementation, SANAV SOFTWARE empowers businesses, institutions, and organizations to achieve scalable and sustainable growth through technology.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading IT solutions provider, follow our journey of innovation and growth.

2012

The Beginning

Founded in Patna, Bihar with a vision to provide affordable and quality IT solutions to local businesses. Started with web development services and small software projects.

2014

Expansion & Growth

Expanded our service portfolio to include custom software development, mobile applications, and IT consultancy. Established our first dedicated office space and hired additional developers.

2016

Industrial Training Division

Launched our Industrial Training division to bridge the gap between academia and industry. Started offering professional training in modern technologies to students and professionals.

2018

MSME Recognition

Registered as an MSME enterprise and received government recognition. This milestone helped us access new opportunities and expand our client base across multiple states.

2020

Digital Transformation Focus

Pivoted to focus on digital transformation solutions during the pandemic. Helped numerous businesses transition to online operations and remote work solutions.

2023

National Recognition

Recognized as one of the fastest-growing IT companies in Eastern India. Expanded our team to 50+ professionals and served 500+ clients across various industries.

Present

Future Ready

Focusing on AI, Machine Learning, and Cloud Solutions. Expanding our footprint with plans to open new offices and develop innovative products for the global market.
